Savings, Costs and Spending

Saving is like keeping a treasure for later adventures. Costs are the coins you trade for things you need. Spending is using your coins to make your day special and exciting.

Savings: is like collecting your magic coins in a special account bank. When you get extra coins, you put them in your account bank for later. It's like a treasure chest for your future adventures!

Costs: are like the magic coins you spend on things you need. When you want to buy something, you use your magic coins to pay for it. It's like trading your coins for something you like or need.

Spending: is when you use your magic coins to get something special or do fun activities. It's like sharing your coins to make your day more exciting. Spelling is about using your coins wisely!

Let's go for that goal!

Sometimes, projects can feel like an exciting puzzle. Each piece is an adventure, and I know it can be challenging at times. But remember, you are the brave adventurers in this story! Here are some magical words to keep in mind when things get a bit tough: Persistency, Imagination and Teamwork!

Step 1: Decide what you want to achieve. It could be finishing a drawing, completing a puzzle, or learning something new. Setting a goal gives you a clear target.

Step 2: Think about the steps you need to take to reach your goal. Break it down into smaller tasks. For example, if your goal is to finish a drawing, the tasks could be sketching, coloring, and adding details.

Step 3: Don't feel rushed. Take your time and complete one task at a time. Celebrate each small achievement along the way. This makes the journey more fun and helps you stay motivated.
