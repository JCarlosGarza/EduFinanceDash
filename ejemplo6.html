Credit and Debit

Understanding Credit and Debt: Learn how to use money wisely and make smart financial choices. Building a strong foundation for a bright financial future!

Types of Cards

Credit and debit cards are financial tools designed to help children and teenagers learn about money management in a controlled and supervised environment.

Basic Debit Card

Features: - Linked to a parent's account. - Limited spending controls and allowances set by parents. - No overdraft protection. - Real-time parental monitoring through apps. - Educational tools for basic financial lessons.

Intermediate Debit Card

Features: - Increased spending limits. - Parental controls for online and in-store purchases. - Introduction of basic ATM access (with withdrawal limits). - Enhanced educational resources for financial literacy. - Some customization options for parents to tailor features.

Advance Debit Card

Features: - Higher spending limits and responsibility. - More flexibility in ATM access. - Introduction of features like savings goals and budgeting tools. - Parental oversight remains, but with gradual independence. - Advanced financial education resources.

Customizable Debit Card

Features: - Customizable features based on individual financial goals. - Higher spending limits and fewer restrictions. - Full ATM access and management of their accounts. - Integration with mobile banking apps for seamless control. - Continued focus on financial literacy and advanced money management.
