Types of candies: Financial Instruments

Financial instruments are various types of contracts or assets (like candies!) that can be traded between parties (like your friends!). This is the reason why they have a price.

Stocks

Imagine stocks as Blue Candy Shares. When you own a Candy Share, you have a piece of a big Candy Company. If the Candy Company does well, your Candy Shares become more valuable. It's like having a share of the magical Candy Market!

Derivatives

Derivatives are like Pink Candy Magic Tricks. They're special contracts that can change in value based on the Candy Market's movements. It's like a magical game where the value of your Candy Magic Trick can go up or down.

Crypto

Crypto is like Magic Light Blue Candy Coins. Instead of regular coins, these are special digital coins that you can use to buy candies in the magical Candy Market. Each coin is like a magical token in the digital world!

NFTs

NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens) are like Unique Green Candy Art. Imagine each piece of candy having its special art or flavor. NFTs are unique digital tokens that show you own a one-of-a-kind piece of Candy Art in the digital world.
