Types of Insurances

Insurance is a way to protect ourselves from unexpected events or emergencies that might cause financial problems. It's like a safety net that helps us when something unexpected happens!

Health Insurance

What It Covers: Doctor visits, medicines, hospital stays, and sometimes dental and eye care.

Cental Insurance

What It Covers: Check-ups, cleanings, and treatments for teeth and gums.

Life Insurance

What It Covers: This one is a bit different. It provides money to the family if something happens to a parent or guardian.

Car Insurance

What It Covers: If the family has a car, this insurance helps pay for damages in case of an accident.

Travel Insurance

What It Covers: Trip cancellations, medical emergencies, lost baggage, and other travel-related incidents.

Pet Insurance

What It Covers: Veterinary expenses for pets, including illness and accidents.

Education Insurance

What It Covers: Investment plans specifically tailored for accumulating funds to cover educational expenses.

Savings Insurance

What It Covers: Save in another currency to preserve the value of your money, choosing the amount and term of your savings to reach your goal.
